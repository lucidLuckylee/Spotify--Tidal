Spotify to Tidal

Getting Started

Transfer your Spotify library to Tidal in three steps:

1
Log into Spotify

Open open.spotify.com and sign in. The extension will detect your session automatically.

2
Log into Tidal

Open listen.tidal.com and sign in. Browse around briefly so the extension can capture your session.

3
Capture & Export

Hit "Capture All" to grab your Spotify library, then export it to Tidal. Tracks are matched automatically.
